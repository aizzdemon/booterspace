How Offline Tailoring Works

Smart local matching engine overview

This workspace operates **entirely inside your browser** without sending data to external AI servers. It uses standard ATS parsing and matching metrics:

  • Keyword Extraction: Analyzes your target requirements for focus skills, adding them dynamically to your customized resume summary and skills inventory.
  • Dynamic Experience Rewriting: Standardize and align experiences with professional metrics matched directly with targeted keyword triggers.
  • Cover Letter Compiler: Structures formal correspondence addressing company managers and mentioning critical skills.
