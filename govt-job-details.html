Eligibility Criteria

  • Law Graduate (LLB) from a recognized University
  • Must be eligible for registration as Advocate in Court
  • Age: Min 21 years | Max 37 years (UR Male) as on 01.08.2026
  • BC/EBC (M/F) & UR Female: up to 40 years
  • SC/ST (M/F): up to 42 years
  • Relaxations for Govt. servants, Ex-Servicemen, PwBD

Selection Process

  • Preliminary Exam — GS: 100 marks + Law: 150 marks (Objective)
  • Main Exam — 8 Descriptive Papers, Total 1000 marks
  • Interview — 100 marks
  • Final merit based on Main Exam + Interview only
  • No negative marking in Prelims (as per notification)
  • Only online applications accepted via BPSC portal
Application Fee: ₹100 (General) + bank charges  |  Mode: Online only  |  Domicile: Bihar residents (for reservation)
